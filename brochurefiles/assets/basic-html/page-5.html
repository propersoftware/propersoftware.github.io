PGI

Machining

| 5

PGI Machining has been very busy over the past few months.

At the IMTS Show in September, Proper purchased a new MCW

Toshiba Milling Machine. It is capable of 2D and 3D machining

vertically and horizontally. In November, a foundation was

poured for the new machine. The machine arrived late

November and was operational the first week of February.

Proper also purchased a new state of

the art tool presetter for greater tool

accuracy and speed. It is as accurate

as any laser and is not subject to all

of the damage when attached to a

machine.

PGIM did well during our ISO audit and did not have any

nonconformances. PGIM now has a safety team in place and we

meet every other week to address any concerns to stay current.

Maintenance is currently painting yellow lines on the floor as

part of our safety improvement.

We recently did two unique retrofits. One was a 1998 Saturn

Fender to run in IACMI’s new 3000 ton Milacron Press. The other

was a 12 year old Cedar Siding Stack Mold for Saint Gobain /

Certainteed.

Steve Vandenberghe

General Manager
