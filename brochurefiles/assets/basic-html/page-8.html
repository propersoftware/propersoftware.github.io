Warren

Proper Polymers is growing again!! This time in the Warren Plant.

The recent expansion adds a 3rd production bay in support of the

Otter Venture Cooler Collection, and the Automotive Lighting High

Volume Dodge Truck components. Otter is expanding the market

for their coolers which is causing quite a stir. The Magneti Marelli,

Automotive Lighting customer has opened a facility in Clarkston, MI

which drove the Housing Parts being localized to our South Eastern

Michigan facility. The addition has given us opportunity to increase

our Otter Cooler output by 40%, and also allows the sales team to

summons more business from the regional areas exterior lighting

customers. This Proper Polymers Group expansion is exciting and a

contributing factor to the Proper Group International’s growth.
