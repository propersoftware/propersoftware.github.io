GIVE YOURSELF

The Saver's Credit could pay you back in retirement – and at tax time.

The Saver’s Credit offers a chance to save for the future and get money back at tax time. Depending

on your income and tax filing status, you could get back up to 50% percent of the first $2,000 you

contribute to your retirement plan (or 50% of the first $4,000 if you are married and file jointly). It

works like a rebate and is worth as much as $1,000 for single filers and $2,000 for married couples

who file jointly. Here’s how much it could mean to you based on your adjusted gross income (AGI)

in 2017:
