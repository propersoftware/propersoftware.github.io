Computer security is ever important in this day and age.

Cybersecurity includes many areas including I/T services to

block harmful emails and virus programs that change daily.

So far this year, our e-mail content filter servers have screened

millions of e-mails into SPAM, or quarantine to help you clear

them without I/T help.

A major step to advance our ability to combat the bad guys is

YOU and your awareness when something seems “fishy”, or

strange. The industry term“Phishing” is used to describe e-mail

fishing for someone to respond. They try to trick you using a

familiar E-mail sender’s name masked as a Proper executive or

your common associates’ names. An easy way to identify a

masked e-mail is look at the real address next to the person’s

name.
